About PNGs

PNG is one of the most commom picture file types, it stands for Portable Network Graphics. PNG files are good since they keep all the details of an image due to lossless compression. Typically they are used to save digital images rather than photo taken with a camera. It is also one of the few image formats that support transparency while retaining a high quality.
